Hotels in Agra Near Taj Mahal

Agra, a beautiful city located in the state of Uttar Pradesh, is an emblem of affluent historical past and architectural brilliance of the Mughal epoch. This magnificent city boasts one of the Seven Wonders of the World – the Taj Mahal. This monument is a symbol of love. Because of the several architecturally wonderful edifices, Agra attracts a horde of tourists round the year from all around the world. To cater to the staying needs of the guests, Agra encompasses an enticing list of hotels.

Whether it is a five-star luxury hotel or a budget one, hotels in Agra render heart-warming amenities and services and make the sojourn relaxing and memorable. In addition, these hotels offer lip-smacking dining options that delight the taste buds of the guests. Whatever is the budget of the traveler, it is certain that he will find a perfect accommodation option in Agra.

The travellers looking for five-star hotels in Agra can choose from properties like Amar Vilas Palace, Taj Gateway, Hotel Trident, etc. The major four-star hotels in the city are Hotel Marina, Hotel Howard Plaza and Hotel Mansingh Palace. The enticing list of three-star hotels in Agra encompasses options like Hotel Taj Vilas, Hotel Pushp Villa, Hotel Royal Residency, etc.
